Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need for tax filing?

Generally, you will need your W-2s or 1099s, records of expenses/deductions, previous year's tax return, and valid identification. For businesses, profit and loss statements and balance sheets are also required.

Do you work with remote clients?

Yes! We utilize secure cloud-based accounting software and video conferencing tools to serve clients globally. You can upload documents securely through our client portal.

How are your fees structured?

We offer both fixed-fee packages for ongoing services (like bookkeeping) and hourly rates for specialized consulting. We provide a transparent quote before starting any work.

Can you help with IRS audits?

Absolutely. We provide full audit representation services, communicating directly with tax authorities on your behalf to resolve issues efficiently and minimize penalties.
